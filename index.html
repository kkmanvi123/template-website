working together to keep the internet alive and accessible, so people worldwide can be informed contributors and creators of the Web. Firefox, developed by Mozilla Corporation, is an open-source web browser known for its emphasis on privacy, security, and customization. With a user-friendly interface, it offers fast and efficient browsing, coupled with default enhanced tracking protection to safeguard user privacy. Supporting a wide range of extensions, Firefox provides a personalized and secure web experience while maintaining a commitment to web standards.
